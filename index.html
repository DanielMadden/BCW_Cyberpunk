A Whole New World

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure story set in Night City, a megalopolis obsessed with power, glamour, and body modification.

Weapons:  Endless arsenal of weapons to choose from
Body Modifications:  Fully customize your abilities and HUD with cybernetic enhancements
Powers: Climb walls, gain super speed, and see enemies through barriers
Socialize: Every interaction with NPCs is recorded and adapted to
Realistic: Every single action takes part in your future (see below)

Features from the future

A game like you've never seen before. Cyberpunk blends RPGs, first-person shooters, open-world exploration, and intense action with a future that adapts to your choices.

Your Choice, Your Destiny.

You have full control over what your life will look like. At the very beginning players can choose, not only the looks of their character, but their backstory as well. From that point on, every single decision made affects how the player will be treated and what events will occur throughout the game.

Want to rush a base with guns blazing? Be our guest. Prefer the more passive path? You can do that too. Just remember that with every action there is an equal and opposing action taken towards that choice.

The possibilities are endless. What destiny shall you choose?
